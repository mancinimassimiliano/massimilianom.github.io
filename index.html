Massimiliano Mancini

I am a PhD student at Department of Computer, Control, and Management Engineering Antonio Ruberti at Sapienza University of Rome, in collaboration with Fondazione Bruno Kessler (Trento, Italy) supervised by Prof. Barbara Caputo and Prof. Elisa Ricci. The focus of my work is domain adaptation/generalization and incremental learning in the context of computer and robot vision.

I received my bachelor degree from University of Perugia (Italy).
