Massimiliano Mancini

I am a PhD student at Sapienza University of Rome in the Department of Computer, Control, and Management Engineering Antonio Ruberti, in collaboration with Fondazione Bruno Kessler (Trento, Italy) supervised by Prof. Barbara Caputo and Prof. Elisa Ricci. I am a member of the Visual and Multimodal Applied Learning Lab (VANDAL) and the Technologies of Vision Lab (TEV).

I received the master degree in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics from Sapienza University of Rome (Italy) in 2016, with maximum grade and excellence mention. Previously, I received the bachelor degree in Computer Science and Electronic Engineering from University of Perugia (Italy) in 2014.

The main goal of my work is developing algorithms for increasing the generalization capabilities of deep architectures. In particular my focus is addressing problems such as domain adaptation/generalization and incremental learning in the context of computer and robot vision.
